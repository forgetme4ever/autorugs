What is EVA mats?

material

EVA mats are all-weather and feature a unique diamond-shaped mesh surface. Because of what, water does not spill when removing the carpet and does not splash when driving. Carpets are waterproof.

difference

Rubber mats bend and climb on the pedals. EVA mats will not allow this! Special Velcro keeps it in one position. If desired, a embossed aluminum thrust bearing is installed for the driver.

colors

EVA car mats can be ordered in individual sizes and in different colors, which can be combined at the buyer's choice with the same colors of the edging polypropylene belt tape.
